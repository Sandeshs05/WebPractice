Our Adventures

Kemmangundi

Karnataka

Kemmangundi, nestled in the Baba Budangiri Range, is a serene hill station in Karnataka, India. Known for its lush landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and the picturesque Z Point, it offers a tranquil retreat amidst the Western Ghats' natural beauty.

Kudremukh

Karnataka

Kudremukh, in Karnataka, India, is a captivating mountain range renowned for its biodiverse landscapes and pristine forests. Home to the Kudremukh National Park, it attracts nature enthusiasts with its lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and captivating trekking trails.

Waynad

Kerala

Wayanad, located in Kerala, India, is a captivating hill station known for its enchanting landscapes, dense forests, and vibrant wildlife. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventurous experiences for every visitor.
